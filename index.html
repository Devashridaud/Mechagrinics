Who Team MechAgrinics ?

Team MechAgrinics, from JSPM's Rajarshi Shahu College of
Engineering, located in Pune, India, is a group of dedicated
students competing in agricultural technology challenges.
Comprised of students from various engineering disciplines,
the team collaborates to design, build, and deploy innovative
farming machinery aimed at enhancing agricultural efficiency
and sustainability. Since its inception, Team MechAgrinics has
consistently excelled in competitions, achieving notable rankings
and earning recognition for its cutting-edge designs and strong
teamwork. The team is driven by a passion for transforming
agriculture through technology and a commitment to
supporting farmers with economically viable solutions.
